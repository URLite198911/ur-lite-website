About UR Lite

UR Lite is a lightweight Windows desktop reader for opening common document, ebook, and comic archive formats without a complicated interface.

It is designed for users who want a simple reader focused on local file viewing. UR Lite opens files on the user's own computer and does not require users to upload documents to the cloud.

What it supports

  • PDF
  • EPUB
  • DOCX
  • TXT
  • CBR
  • CBZ

Helper software

DOCX Original Layout depends on LibreOffice being installed locally. If LibreOffice is not available, UR Lite opens DOCX files in HTML/Reflow View.

Some archive formats may depend on installed helper tools. CBZ support may use 7-Zip, and CBR support may require WinRAR or compatible RAR support.

For safety, download helper software only from the official websites linked in the README.
